Rating Methodology

Each beet salad is evaluated on a 5-star scale across the following categories:

  • Overall: The complete experience - taste, presentation, value, and satisfaction
  • Ingredients: Quality, freshness, and variety of components
  • Sweet: Balance of natural sweetness from beets and other elements
  • Citrus: Brightness and acidity from citrus notes
  • Crunch: Textural contrast from nuts, seeds, or crispy elements
